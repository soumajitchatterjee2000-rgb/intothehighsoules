Top Destinations

Darjeeling

The Queen of Hills. Famous for its tea gardens, the Toy Train (UNESCO site), and breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga.

Manali

A high-altitude Himalayan resort town. Ideal for backpacking, skiing, and trekking through the Rohtang Pass.

Kerala

God's Own Country. Explore serene backwaters on houseboats, lush palm-lined beaches, and Ayurvedic wellness.

Arunachal Pradesh

The Land of the Rising Sun. Discover hidden monasteries, tribal heritage, and the pristine Tawang Valley.

Goa

Sun, Sand, and Sea. From vibrant nightlife in the North to peaceful, secluded beaches in the South.

Mumbai

The City of Dreams. Experience the colonial architecture, the iconic Gateway of India, and the Marine Drive sunset.
